Neu Berliner

Inspired heavily by Berliner, another framework doing the same thing. The idea is to write quick web apps in Coffeescript.

Sample

Here's a simple example:

app = require 'neu-berliner'

app.get '/', -> 'Hello, world!'

app.run 4567

Fairly simple, as you can see. You can find some more details here.

Future plans

None, for now, although the library may be tested with io.js, and possibly even extended, to make sure it's compatible.
